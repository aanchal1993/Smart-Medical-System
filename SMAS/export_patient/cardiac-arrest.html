SMART MEDICAL ACCESS SYSTEM

  • SIGNS OF CARDIAC ARREST

    Sudden loss of responsiveness

    No response to tapping on shoulder

    Nausea

    Difficulty in breathing

  • WHAT TO DO ??

    Call For help

    If  person is not breathing, give CPR

    Push hard and push fast until person starts breathing

    Use AED as soon as it arrives

Sign Out
