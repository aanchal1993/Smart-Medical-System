APEX HOSPITAL

Apex Hospital is located at Prime Location of the City, This hospital was started in 1967 by Dr. Harinder Kaur under the name of 'Dr. Harinder Kaur Hospital'. She was a renowned & well known name in the field of Obstetrics / Gynaecology & Infertility Management. Her legacy is being carried forward by her children under the name of Apex Hospital & Maternity Home. From a 10 bedded nursing home it has now been expanded to a 60 bedded multi specialty hospital with specialties of General & Laparoscopic Surgery, Urology, Endourology, Andrology, Gynae, Laparoscopic & Endoscopic Surgery, Infertility Management/ Test Tube baby (I.U.I/ IVF/ I.C.S.I), Obstetrics, gynaecology, Orthopedics, Joint Replacement Surgeries, Arthroscopy, Spine Surgery & Allied specialities/ super-specialities.
